If you are interested in finding out more about the services please don't hesitate to contact me and I can see how I can help you.

Enquiries

So the first initially enquiry is done over the phone and that normally regards availability, so when phoning its best to have these details

  • Date
  • One or Two man team
  • Location from and to
  • Rough estimate of size of the removal

Once I have this information I will either be available or not. If I am available I will require all the above information in a text message to properly confirm and book you in.

Bookings

Once I send you a price, please don't forget to confirm. also a message like "Please book me in for then" as "ok" have left customers thinking they have booked when "ok" is not a confirming answer.
