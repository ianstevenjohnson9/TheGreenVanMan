Moving Tips

Make Moving Easy

Organization is the key for an efficient move.

Boxing up your possessions

  1. Make sure the box is big enough for the all the contents and is able to be closed.
  2. Label box to the destination as this will reduce asking where boxes go, resulting in a more efficient removal.

Sofas

  1. Removal of the feet makes the sofa easier to get out of the property and into the next one.
  2. Removal of cushions if possible.

Flat pack furniture/beds

  1. Remembering whether you built it up in the room is important.
  2. Taking the doors off wardrobes does make a difference.
  3. Dismantling is always the preferred option as that is the way it came in.

Summary

So when thinking about moving consider closed boxes and labels, taking the feet off the sofas, and flat pack furniture dismantling. These methods create an easier move for all parties.
